Make An Impact

Support Education, Healing, and Families

A-SALEM works to keep children in school, support women and families, and provide safe opportunities in the Democratic Republic of Congo. Your help makes it possible for children to pay school fees and continue learning.

Why Help Matters

Without your support children will go hungry profoundly altering their development and well-being Malnutrition impairs physical growth, damages the immune system, and causes lasting cognitive and / or behavorial challenges.

Education is not free in our region. Families must pay school fees, uniforms, books, and supplies. When they cannot afford these costs, boys and girls risk repeating grades or dropping out entirely.

A-SALEM helps children continue their studies by funding school fees, uniforms, transport, and learning materials. We support women and families with counseling, economic training, and community resources.
