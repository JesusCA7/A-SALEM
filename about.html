Our Story

Founded in 2002 by Alice Apendeke, A-SALEM is a grassroots, women-led organization based in the Democratic Republic of Congo. The name A-SALEM is derived from the Hebrew word Salem, meaning "peace."

Our Mission

True to its name, A-SALEM was born from a deep personal mission to bring peace, dignity, and support to those who have none.
